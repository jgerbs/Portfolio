Why Is This Slow?

A naive brute force attack must try every possible combination, even if the correct PIN is very early. This makes the technique slow and easy to predict, so attackers usually combine it with smarter methods.

What You Will See

  • A steady count moving from 0000 upward
  • The number of attempts increasing over time
  • More time needed when the PIN is closer to 9999
