Why Is This Slow?

Naive brute force has to check every possible combination, even if the correct PIN is very early in the sequence. Attackers rarely rely on this method alone because it is slow and predictable.

What You'll See

  • A steady, sequential count from 0000 upward
  • A growing number of attempts
  • Longer search times for PINs near 9999

This simulation runs locally in your browser and is safe, educational, and does not store any data.
