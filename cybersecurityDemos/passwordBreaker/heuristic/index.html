How Heuristics Work

People tend to choose predictable PINs. A heuristic attack takes advantage of this by checking the most likely patterns first.

  • Common PINs such as 1234, 1111, or 0000
  • Repetition patterns like aaaa, abab, or aabb
  • Birth years often between 1970 and 2025
  • Simple number patterns like 2468 or 2580

Since many people fall into these patterns, heuristic attacks often succeed very quickly. Only after trying all smart guesses does the algorithm switch to a full brute force search.

If this demo finds your PIN early, it is a good sign that your PIN is predictable. Consider choosing something more random.
