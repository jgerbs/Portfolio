How Heuristics Work

Humans are predictable. Heuristic attacks use this fact by testing:

  • Common PINs (e.g., 1234, 1111, 0000)
  • Repetition patterns (aaaa, abab, aabb)
  • Birth years (1970–2025 ranges)
  • Simple numeric patterns (2468, 2580)

These guesses succeed very quickly on real users. Only after exhausting smart guesses does the algorithm switch to a full brute-force search.

If this attack finds your PIN early, consider switching to something more random.
